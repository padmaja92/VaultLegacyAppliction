Welcome to Vault Legacy

Vault Legacy is a next-generation unified wealth management platform designed to consolidate all your financial assets—across banks, brokerages, and investment platforms—into one secure digital hub.

From checking accounts, savings, CDs, and credit cards to 401(k)s, Roth IRAs, stocks, bonds, and crypto portfolios, Vault Legacy provides users with a single, encrypted interface to view, analyze, and manage their entire financial landscape.

The platform empowers individuals and families to take control of their wealth by simplifying complexity, offering transparency, and ensuring that no asset is ever overlooked.
